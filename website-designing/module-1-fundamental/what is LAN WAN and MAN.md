        1. what is LAN WAN and MAN
        
        1. LAN (Local Area Network)

        A LAN connects computers and devices within a small area such as a home, office, school, or building.

        Example
        Computers connected in a computer lab
        Home Wi-Fi network

        2. MAN (Metropolitan Area Network)

         A MAN connects multiple LANs across a city or metropolitan area.

        Example
        Network connecting different branches of a bank within a city
    

        3. WAN (Wide Area Network)

        A WAN connects computers and networks over very large distances, such as countries or continents.

        Example
        Corporate network connecting offices worldwide
